"I have reviewed the proposed amendments to Section 73 concerning the acceptance of deposits from the public. While the intent to streamline compliance is appreciated, the new provisions for penalizing minor procedural lapses appear overly stringent and could disproportionately affect small enterprises. I propose that a tiered penalty structure be introduced, differentiating between willful non-compliance and unintentional errors. Furthermore, the timeline for reporting seems too short, especially for companies with decentralized operations. An extension to 90 days would be more practical."
